A Call to Arms.

For the past few decades, efforts have been focused on combating Earth's resource depletion crisis.

We continue to exhaust our resources and approach the topic as if it is merely a war to be fought. Guerilla warfare is carried out in the guise of startup companies, entrepreneur conferences, award ceremonies and scientific campaigns.

We applaud those attempting to solve the puzzle — and rightfully so — but it seems that we've gotten lost in all of the applause and have forgotten what made us clap to begin with.

As a civilization, we are constantly advancing and improving upon the methods of yesterday. If there are levels to the development of a civilization, then we can be classified as just entering the 2nd floor. In this skyscraper, however, the same staircase cannot be used twice.

Since we do not want to receive the same result over and over again we must change our methodology. As the stairs get steeper we will switch out our sandals for boots so that we can climb higher to reach even more floors than before.

Hydroxy's Staircase:

There is a huge water crisis that is going to hit us sooner than later. Some of the solutions that are being created in response include the desalination of seawater and recycling wastewater. The problem with these solutions is that they are temporary and not effective enough.
